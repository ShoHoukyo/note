図解 保険

p43 予定利率↑ affects rate used by insurance-company and product, overrall trend: 保険料↓

p164 maybe foreign insurance-company operating in JP has cheaper product

p55 定期保険 自動更新 (only 2 weeks notice period, pay attention) low price + 自動更新 price up vs. medium price over long period

  • better deal if death in early years
  • or consider early-break-contract cost

P92 consider terms when need to increase coverage after some years

P119 通院 特約 (be careful, lots of cases in non-payment) P124 there is limit on out-of-pocket payment monthly for illness already in usual health insurance, so not much point buying separate one?

P130 重度疾病 pay once diagnosed, this is necessary companion to life-insurance

P138 set coverage amount 加入金額の目安 don't consider JP-home-loan (if any), usually there is separate life-insurance to cover it

P151 maybe cheaper to buy online

P172 insurance legal-term is hard to understand (especially for foreigner) and JP-insurance-law allows changing terms in case of insurance-company-bankrupt so aim to buy popular product from good insurance-companies

P209 lots of non-payment situations including:

  • https://www.jili.or.jp/knows_learns/q_a/life_insurance/165.html

  • 戦争その他の変乱、地震、噴火または津波によるとき

P251 to assess risk of insurance-company bankrupt:

  • 保険会社 ソルベンシー・マージン （支払余力） at least 200%
  • 保険金支払能力 格付 but see book 生命保険で損をしたくないならこの1冊

  • have to apply to insurance company, follow instructions with paperwork
  • write to will the process
  • apply within 3 years of death (do check contract)
