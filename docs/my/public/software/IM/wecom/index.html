1. How to get a WeCom Employee Account You cannot register an employee account yourself on the web. WeCom (WeChat Work) is an invitation-only system for employees.

  • The Process: An administrator from a company must create the account for you and send you an invitation (via SMS, Email, or WeChat).
  • Your Action: You simply accept the invitation to "Join" the organization. e.g. login with phone number or email (no need to use personal wechat)

2. Can you use it without the Mobile App? No. Using WeCom solely via a web browser or PC client without a mobile app is effectively impossible due to their security model.

  • The "Scan" Barrier: The WeCom Web interface and PC Desktop software require you to scan a QR code using the WeCom mobile app (or sometimes WeChat mobile app) to log in. There is no "username/password" login option for the PC version that completely bypasses the mobile device check.
  • Web Portal: The web version (web.work.weixin.qq.com) functions as a mirror of the mobile app and will disconnect if the mobile session is inactive for too long.

3. Do you need a "real" phone number? Yes.

  • Verification: Virtual numbers (VoIP like Google Voice, Skype) are frequently blocked by Tencent's anti-fraud systems. You likely will not receive the verification SMS.
  • Binding: Your account is permanently tied to a mobile number for identity verification.

You would install the WeCom app inside the emulator, receive the SMS on a dumb-phone/real SIM, verify it inside the emulator, and then use the emulator as your "phone" to authorize your PC sessions.

will I get private messages if I login wecom with wechat (private)?

No, you will not get private WeChat messages in your WeCom login.

WeCom (WeChat Work) is designed to separate personal (WeChat) and professional (WeCom) communications.

  • Segregation: WeCom is a separate app/platform. When you use your personal WeChat account for WeCom login (authentication), you are essentially using it as an identity provider, not merging the communication history. Your personal WeChat chats remain exclusively in the WeChat app.
  • Interoperability: WeCom allows employees to chat with external WeChat users (customers/clients). However, these specific chats are considered business-related communications on the WeCom side.
  • Company Data: Communications within WeCom are often subject to company oversight/monitoring and compliance archiving, especially in regulated industries [2.2, 3.5]. This is a core reason why WeCom keeps its data separate from personal WeChat messages, which are generally not monitored by the employer [3.3].

Data Shared during Login/Binding:

When you link your WeChat account to WeCom, only specific personal data is shared for profile setup and authentication:

  • Name

  • Profile picture

  • Mobile phone number

  • WeChat/Weixin friends list (if you grant permission to add external contacts) 2025-12-03 can not deny this request?

  • WeChat/Weixin ID

Your personal chat history/messages are not transferred to the WeCom platform or made accessible to your employer's admin console.
