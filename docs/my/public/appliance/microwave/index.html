microwave

  • check on size to fit on top of fridge (good: 44W x 33D x 25.5H)
  • 庫内サイズ（mm） enough: 幅×奥行き×最大高さ：306×307×208
  • power cord length better longer than 1.4 meter
  • easy buttons should be best, plain dial is hard to be exact
  • light weight (under 10 kg)
  • about right: 3 power output: 200W, 500W, 700W
  • no turn-table, which is easy to break
  • buy 50Hz（東日本地域）, as dual (50/60Hz) usually has limited power for 50Hz

multi-function ones may be heavy, not as good as 2 separate: oven (light), microwave-oven

  • plain oven is light
  • may use at the same time
